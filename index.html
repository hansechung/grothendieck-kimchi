Organized by Mike Pierce

Introduction

This is going to be an English translation of Alexander Grothendieck’s writing on kimchi. I have very little to do with the words; I’m simply crowdsourcing the translation on github. Please feel free to contribute anything you can! A scan of the original French can be found here on the website of Thibault Damour. If you don't know what kimchi is, then watch this.
