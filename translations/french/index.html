Grothendieck’s Musings on Kimchi

Organized by Mike Pierce

This is a working English translation of Alexander Grothendieck’s essay on kimchi. I have very little to do with the words; I’m simply organizing/​crowd­sourcing the trans­lation via GitHub. Please feel free to contribute anything you can! A scan of the original essay in French can be found here. If you don't know what kimchi is, then I encourage you to watch this. The typesetting of this page is adapted from Tufte CSS.
